Services

Dental fillings

ACHIEVE A SMOOTH SMILE WITH OUR GENERAL DENTISTRY SERVICES.Dental fillings are done for the carious teeth (decayed teeth).Fillings are available as tooth colored fillings.These filling prevent further cavity progression of the tooth.

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is a dental procedure to remove inflamed pulp or damaged pulp,which is then carefully cleaned and disinfected.Root canal treatment is done to save the tooth so the tooth can be useful in chewing and function.

Crows

Crown is a tooth-shaped cap that replaces your normal tooth above the gum line. You may need a crown to support a weak tooth or root canal treated teeth and make your tooth better in chewing.

Bridges and Crowns

Dental bridges replace missing teeth. Specifically, they can replace one tooth or a row of missing teeth.As the name implies, this appliance literally “bridges the gap” in your smile. In our clinic we can create custom bridges that match the shade of your natural teeth.

Dental Implants

A Dental implants is a structure that replaces a missing tooth.they are made up of titanium and are inserted into your jaw bone. They are replacement roots for missing teeth often comfortable. Replacing a lost tooth with a dental implant can improve the patient's quality of life and health.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth can be discolored due to smoking, tea,coffee. Bleaching can be done by either carbamide peroxide or hydrogen peroxide.The peroxide oxidising agents penetrates the rod like crystalline structures and break down the stain.

Kids dentistry

Fillings for decayed teeth.Preventive dental care including cleaning and fluoride treatments, Habit counseling ,example, thumb sucking,mouth breathing,lip biting.Early assessment and treatment for straightening teeth and correcting an improper bite (orthodontics)

Orthodontic treatment

Orthodontic treatment will straighten the teeth or move them into a better position. This can improve their appearance and the way the teeth bite together, while also making them easier to clean.

Invisalign treatment

Invisalign is the brand name of a type of thin clear aligner used in orthodontic treatment. The aligner is made from a flexible thermoplastic material named SmartTrack.

Wisdom tooth extraction

Wisdom teeth that have become impacted or haven't fully broken through the surface of the gum can cause dental problems. Food and bacteria can get trapped around the edge of the wisdom teeth, causing a build-up of plaque, which can lead to tooth decay,gum disease,pericoronitis and cellulitis.Wisdom teeth removal is usually recommended when the teeth is inside the bone and not fully erupting or when it damages adjacent tooth.

Dentures(False teeth)

Dentures are removable false teeth made of acrylic (plastic), nylon or metal. They fit snugly over the gums to replace missing teeth and eliminate potential problems caused by gaps.

Sterilisation protocol

Dental instruments used in examination and procedures that cannot be discarded are thoroughly cleaned and disinfected., the instruments are then sterilised using FDA approved machines, such as dental autoclave sterilizers.
